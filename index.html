Deforestation

Deforestation or forest clearance is the removal of a forest or stand of trees from land that is then converted to non-forest use. Deforestation can involve conversion of forest land to farms, ranches, or urban use. The most concentrated deforestation occurs in tropical rainforests. If we don’t stop deforestation, we will be Gone forever. Trees pull carbon dioxide out of the atmosphere and when we cut trees, they put it back out. This is a deadly gas, that is a part of global warming. Try to donate as much as you can!

Refugees

A refugee is somebody forced to flee their home county do to war or terror. A refugee is somebody that has been through a lot, death, loss of family members, and altogether life. A war is a very hard thing to overcome. When a refugee flees they have to come to a dirty refugee camp. Where they could wait for months to years to be accepted into a bigger country.
